Viviana runs Advancing Arts Forward, a movement to advance equity, inclusion and social justice through the arts.

Artistically, Yura Sapi works in mediums such as poetry, photography, jewelry making, filmmaking, and more.

As an arts manager, they currently lead marketing efforts for Drom NYC, a live world music venue in the East Village, NYC and are a Steering Committee Member of the Latinx Theatre Commons along with other consulting work with Advancing Arts Forward.

As an educator, they teach Theatre Organization and Management at CUNY Baruch College and faciliate other workshops with Advancing Arts Forward.

Viviana is a Cycle IV fellow of the Caribbean Cultural Center African Diaspora Institute's Innovative Cultural Advocacy Fellowship and part of artEquity's 2018 National Facilitator Training cohort. They also hold an MFA in Performing Arts Management from Brooklyn College and a BFA in Theatre Arts from Boston University.
